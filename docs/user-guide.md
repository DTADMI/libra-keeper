# User Documentation

Welcome to LibraKeeper! This guide will help you understand how to use the system as a regular user.

## Getting Started
1. **Register/Login**: Use your email or Google account to sign in.
2. **Browse**: Explore the collection on your dashboard.
3. **Search**: Use the search bar to find specific items.

## Borrowing Items
- Click on an item to see its details.
- If available, click "Borrow" to send a request to the administrator.
- You'll receive an email notification when your request is approved or rejected.
- View your active loans in the "My Loans" section.

## Interactions
- **Like**: Heart your favorite items to save them.
- **Comment**: Share your thoughts or reviews on items.
- **Suggest**: If you want something added to the collection, use the "Suggestions" page.
- **Report**: Found an item that's missing? Use the "Report Missing" button on the item page.

## Messaging
- Need to talk to the owner? Use the "Messages" section to start a conversation.
